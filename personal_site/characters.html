Characters and Roles

Tanks

Tanks are durable frontline heroes who absorb massive amounts of damage, create space for their team, and disrupt enemy positions. They excel at controlling chokepoints, blocking attacks, and leading pushes to secure objectives. A good tank anchors the team and turns the tide of battle by protecting weaker allies.

Captain America uses his shield to defend allies and lead the charge.

Doctor Strange bends reality to shield his team from harm.

Healers

Healers play a vital support role, restoring health to keep teammates in the fight longer and sustaining offensive and defensive pushes. They often stay behind the frontlines, positioning carefully to avoid being targeted while providing healing bursts and buffs that can swing battles in their teamâ€™s favor. Skilled healers prioritize targets, manage cooldowns, and help maintain team cohesion.

Cloak and Dagger combine light and darkness to heal and protect allies.

Jeff the Land Shark boosts morale and provides surprising support on the battlefield.

DPS

DPS heroes specialize in dealing high amounts of damage quickly, focusing on eliminating key enemy targets such as healers or squishy backliners. They often rely on positioning, flanking, and burst damage to gain the upper hand. DPS players are responsible for securing eliminations, breaking enemy lines, and pressuring opponents to create openings for their team.

Hawkeye picks off enemies from afar with unmatched precision.

Squirrel Girl overwhelms foes with her unbeatable squirrel army.
