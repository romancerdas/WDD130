Welcome to Doctor Doom’s Encyclopedia

Doctor Doom’s Encyclopedia is a fan-made site dedicated to the fast-growing competitive shooter Marvel Rivals. Released in 2024, Marvel Rivals has quickly gained a strong following thanks to its dynamic gameplay, destructible environments, and diverse roster of iconic Marvel heroes and villains. Whether you’re here for lore, strategies, or current meta breakdowns — you’re in the right place.

Marvel Rivals isn’t just another competitive shooter — it’s an ever-evolving battleground where iconic heroes and villains clash in ways only possible within the Marvel Universe. Each match is a fast-paced dance of power and strategy, where the environment itself becomes a weapon, and teamwork makes all the difference between victory and defeat. With destructible maps, dynamic objectives, and a roster that’s constantly expanding, the game invites players to experiment with new tactics and adapt on the fly. Whether you’re a fan of high-flying brawlers or calculated tacticians, there’s always a new playstyle to master and a rival to outwit. Our goal is to help you navigate this chaos with in-depth guides, curated strategies, and practical tips that make every match more rewarding — for solo players and coordinated squads alike.

What You'll Find Here

Here, you’ll find quick lore summaries of popular heroes, breakdowns of gameplay strategies that shape matches, and overviews of core character roles. Everything is designed to be clear and useful, so you can spend less time sifting through patch notes and more time winning matches.
