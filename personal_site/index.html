Welcome to Doctor Doom’s Encyclopedia

Doctor Doom’s Encyclopedia is a fan-made site dedicated to the fast-growing competitive shooter Marvel Rivals. Released in 2024, Marvel Rivals has quickly gained a strong following thanks to its dynamic gameplay, destructible environments, and diverse roster of iconic Marvel heroes and villains. Whether you’re here for lore, strategies, or current meta breakdowns — you’re in the right place.

The game blends team-based action with character-driven abilities, appealing to both Marvel fans and players of competitive games alike. This site was created to help players better understand the game’s evolving landscape — not just in terms of how to play, but why certain characters and strategies rise to the top. It’s more than a wiki; it’s your guide to staying one step ahead.

What You'll Find Here

Here, you’ll find quick lore summaries of popular heroes, breakdowns of gameplay strategies that shape matches, and overviews of core character roles. Everything is designed to be clear and useful, so you can spend less time sifting through patch notes and more time winning matches.
